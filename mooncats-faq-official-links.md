MoonCats are an NFT unlike any other.  Unlike most collections, where rarity is crystal clear and determined at the point of minting, 4 billion *potential* MoonCats were generated prior to the contract going live, of which 25 440 were eventually rescued (minted.)  Understanding MoonCats does take a little time, so we've prepared answers to some of the most common questions we get right here.
MoonCats is an Early NFT, and is a pioneer of on-chain generation, fair distribution, and user customization.

**I THOUGHT ALL MOONCATS WERE RESCUED, WHY HAVE SOME BEEN RECENTLY MINTED?**
MoonCats pre-date the ERC-721 standard, and are instead closer to an ERC-20 token.  When they are placed in the official Acclimated wrapper, OpenSea "sees" them for the first time, so considers it the mint date.  The MoonCat's metadata will show what year it was rescued.

**BUT WAIT, THERE'S NOT 25 440 ACCLIMATED MOONCATS?!**
MoonCats are currently split between the Acclimated Wrapper, the Unofficial Wrapper, and Wild MoonCats.

**ARE UNOFFICIAL WRAPPED MOONCATS REAL MOONCATS?**
Yes!  These MoonCats were placed in an old wrapper before the official wrapper was released.  However, this wrapper has few features and will not be compatible with future ponderware or community developed projects.

**OH NO! I BOUGHT A MOONCAT IN AN OLD UNOFFICIAL WRAPPER/MY MOONCAT ISN’T WRAPPED, HOW DO I CONVERT IT?**
The MoonCats Acclimator is here, which includes instructions: https://mooncat.community/acclimator

**WHAT DO PEOPLE MEAN WHEN THEY SAY A "WILD" MOONCAT?**
MoonCats pre-date the ERC-721 standard and exist as ERC-20 tokens.  We generally refer to these totally unwrapped MoonCats as "Wild" -- as opposed to those in the official ERC-721/ERC998 compliant "Acclimated" wrapper.  As of January 22, 2022 there are slightly less than 4900 wild MoonCats.

**CAN MOONCATS BE NAMED?**
Yes, but only once, so choose carefully!! Currently, MoonCats must be renamed and the contract called manually, but a system to simplify this process is coming shortly.
Feeling brave?  Here’s how you name your MoonCat:
Step 1: https://etherscan.io/token/0x60cd862c9c687a9de49aecdc3a99b74a4fc54ab6#writeContract, function #7, get your Moon Cat ID from above
Step 2: Use https://web3-type-converter.onbrn.com/ 
Step 3: Select Convert to "bytes32/hex"
Step 4: Enter desired name in the top white box
Step 5: Enter content in bottom white box in the catName (bytes32) field.
Step 6: Enter Moon Cat ID in the catId (bytes5)
Step 7: Click Write and pay gas (cost depending on price of gwei)
Step 8: After Transaction posted, check this link, function #6 and enter Moon Cat ID: https://etherscan.io/token/0x60cd862c9c687a9de49aecdc3a99b74a4fc54ab6#readContract

**WHAT DOES THE IsNamed TRAIT "INVALID UTF8" MEAN? IS IT RARE?**
Some MoonCats were named with charcters outside of the UTF-8 unicode standard.  These will not display correctly in marketplaces such as OpenSea, so we've replaced their names with a single question mark and a trait to indicate they have unreadable names.  
More info on UTF-8: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UTF-8

**WHAT DOES THE IsNamed TRAIT "REDACTED" MEAN? IS IT RARE?**
Under extremely rare circumstances, the ponderware team may deem a name is wildly inappropriate; in such unusual circumstances, we may choose to hide the names of MoonCats on marketplaces, and use this trait to indicate they have a potentially offensive name.
