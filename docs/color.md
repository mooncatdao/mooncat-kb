# MoonCat Color Labels

For derived human-facing color labels, use `data/color-classification.json` and read [Color Classification](color-classification.md). The source-backed raw trait fields remain `hueInt`, `hueName`, `pale`, and `genesis`.

These labels are for search, filtering, and display only. They do not prove palettes, RGB/hex values, rendering, rarity, or canonical on-chain trait vocabulary. For the broader source and color-math boundary, see `data/color-hues.json` and `docs/color-math.md`.
